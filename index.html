About Us

Developing Leaders for Liberia and Africa

Tolbert Innovation Hub is a youth-focused innovation and empowerment organization committed to transforming lives through education, skills development, technology, and opportunity access.

Inspired by the legacy of President William V. S. Tolbert, the Hub was established to create a future where every Liberian—regardless of background—has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

We believe that innovation, knowledge sharing, and human resource development are key drivers of sustainable national development.

Vision

A Liberia where every individual has the opportunity to reach their full potential, where innovation thrives, and where knowledge is shared by all.

Mission

To foster innovation, education, and human resource development in Liberia and Africa, empowering young people to become leaders and changemakers.

Our Services

Comprehensive Programs for Growth and Opportunity

We offer services designed to empower individuals, strengthen communities, and drive sustainable development.

Scholarship Opportunities

We connect Liberian students to international scholarship and study-abroad opportunities, enabling access to quality higher education and global exposure.

Skill Development Programs

We provide practical, job-ready training programs that equip youths with in-demand skills relevant to today's labor market and entrepreneurship ecosystem.

Global Healthcare Access

We facilitate connections to quality medical treatment options in India, helping individuals and families access specialized healthcare services abroad.
