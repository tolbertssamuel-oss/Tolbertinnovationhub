TOEFL Overview

TOEFL iBT evaluates academic English readiness across Reading, Listening, Speaking, and Writing.

Test Format

  • Reading passages and question sets
  • Listening lectures and conversations
  • Speaking timed responses
  • Writing integrated + independent tasks

Scoring

Each section is scored 0–30 with total score up to 120.

Preparation Strategy

  • Build grammar/vocabulary base
  • Practice integrated skill tasks
  • Use timed tests and targeted review
