Why separate tracks?

  • IELTS Writing Task 1/2 strategy is different from TOEFL Integrated/Independent writing.
  • IELTS Speaking is face-to-face, while TOEFL Speaking is computer-based timed response.
  • Scoring and test-day pacing are not the same, so preparation remains exam-specific.
