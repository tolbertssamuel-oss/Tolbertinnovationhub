FAQ: IELTS & TOEFL Prep

How do I begin if I am a complete beginner?

Start with the Listening and Reading cards above, then move to Writing and Speaking. Use the dashboard to track steady weekly progress.

Can I practice without paid materials?

Yes. We include trusted free resources, video lessons, downloadable PDF packs, and in-browser tools for daily training.

How does the scoring work?

Reading and Listening mini-tests are auto-scored instantly and synced to your local dashboard progress records.

Does this work on mobile phones?

Yes. The classroom uses responsive card layouts, mobile-friendly navigation, and touch-friendly controls.
