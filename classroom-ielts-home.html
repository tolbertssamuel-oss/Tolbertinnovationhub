IELTS Overview

IELTS measures Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking skills for academic and migration goals.

Test Format

  • Listening (4 sections)
  • Reading (3 passages)
  • Writing (Task 1 + Task 2)
  • Speaking (Part 1, 2, 3 interview)

Scoring

Band scores are reported from 0 to 9 for each skill and overall.

Preparation Strategy

  • Foundation (grammar + vocabulary)
  • Skill drills (daily rotation)
  • Timed mock practice + error logs
